Privacy Policy for Alarm6AM

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Alarm6AM, developed by Yusuf Özcan, handles your information.

1. Data Collection

Alarm6AM is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, location, or contacts.

2. Permissions and Sensor Data

The App requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Motion Sensors (CoreMotion): Used exclusively for the "Shake Mission." This data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is never stored or transmitted.
  • Notifications & Audio: Used to trigger alarms and play sounds at your scheduled times.
  • Camera: If used for QR/Barcode missions, the camera feed is processed locally and no images are saved.

3. Local Storage

All alarm settings, focus sessions, and analytics data are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove all this data permanently.
